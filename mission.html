Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

"Empowering Communities through Refreshing Change"

Vision Statement

"Empowering Futures, Refreshing Lives: Icebreakers Foundation envisions a world where revitalization fosters sustainable growth, education, and well-being in communities globally. Through innovation, sustainability, and social responsibility, we aim to forge strategic partnerships that catalyze positive change. Our expanded focus includes youth and adult education, promoting training and development in technology, public service, and media, alongside providing food aid, human services, and job training."

Contribute to the Icebreakers Foundation

We are guided by principles of innovation, sustainability, and unwavering social responsibility. Our goal is to forge strategic partnerships that catalyze positive change.
